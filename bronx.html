What they say: In the city that never sleeps we think it's best to live life without resolutions. We say follow your dreams by making plans, not resolutions. Regrets? Sure, we've had a few, but this beer isn't one of them. This IPA brings hops aromas & bitterness in a big way - a variety of hops create a spectrum of aromas ranging from citrus, pine, musky, tropical fruit to lemon grass. With a 7.6% ABV and a heavy hop guarantee this is the ideal beer for those who live life with No Resolutions.
