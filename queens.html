SingleCut BeerSmiths

What they say: "Located in Astoria, Most of our beers tend to be named after our favorite musicians, though we have been known to get more creative. FOr examply, “19-33” is our address, and coincidentally, the year prohibition ended.”.

What Sara Says: 19-33 is SingleCut's flagship pilsner, described as a Czech/German Pilsner mashup brewed with authentic ingredients from both regions. For a pilsner, this beer was very full bodied with a wide variety of flavors. The hop was a little stonger than I expected for a pilsner and it has a pretty pronounced earthy/spicy quality. My biggest qualm is that the head dissipated almost immediately. Overall, it is an enjoyable pilsner with a little more pizzaz than your standard light beer. It's refreshing for a hot summer's day and I would definitely enjoy it again.

Rating: 4 out of 5 beers
